FAQ

Video

Q. Do you think I can practice music games with an app that just plays videos?
You can do the following things to improve your practice.
● No pausing by tapping the screen during playback
● You can set the playback range
● You can adjust the playback speed
● Video expansion, contraction and movement is possible

Just tapping the screen while playing the part you're weak in is good enough practice 💪
So start practicing today ♪~

Q. Can I orient it vertically?
"Video Selection" You can change the orientation by tapping the "Device is rotating" icon in the right upper corner of the screen.

Q. Can you display touch?
You can't do it with this app.
In the device's Settings app, go to Developer Options and turn on "Show Taps".

Q. Is it possible to make a touch sound?
Settings can be made from "Common settings > Touch on video > Touch sound" from the upper right of the video list screen.
There are various kinds of touch sounds, so please choose the one you like ♪~

Q. Can I adjust the volume of the touch sound?
Settings can be made from "Common settings > Touch on video > Volume" from the upper right of the video list screen.

Q. I'm having trouble with the tap position and pause button being in the same place in some play videos. Can you fix this problem?
You can change the pause button to a long tap.
The button is changed by selecting "Folder Settings > Pause Button" from the upper right button on the video list screen, and then selecting long tap ♪~

Q. Can you fix audio delay?
The audio delay can be adjusted in 0.01 second increments from the video screen.
Please see the separate page for details.

Q. Is there a function to fast forward/rewind for a few seconds?
Yes, you can. You can set it from "Folder settings > Fast Forward/Rewind button" from the upper right button on the video list screen.
You can also specify the number of seconds, so try adjusting it ♪~

Q. Can I reposition or hide the rewind/fast forward button?
The position cannot be changed, but it can be hidden.
You can set it from "Folder settings > Fast Forward/Rewind button" from the upper right button on the video list screen.

Q. Can I delete videos from my device or GoogleDrive after adding a video?
If deleted, the video will no longer play in the application.
This application only reads videos from external sources.

Q. Can I increase the FPS of the video?
This is possible if the FPS of the video is high, the FPS limit of the device is also high, and the device is Android 12 or higher.
Please change it from "Common Settings > Video > fps Limit" from the upper right corner of the video list screen.

Requests/bugs

Q. Where do I make my request?
Please send us your request from "Contact" in the app. We can't accept all requests, but we will consider them.
Sometimes Google Play reviews fail to capture the intent and are sent off, so we recommend that you communicate at the feedback site.

Q. Where do I report bugs?
Please send us your request from "Contact" in the app. We can't accept all requests, but we will consider them.
Sometimes Google Play reviews fail to capture the intent and are sent off, so we recommend that you communicate at the feedback site.

Q. YouTube videos are no longer playing in the app. Is this a bug?
It's most likely a bug. Please contact us from "Feedback" in the upper right corner of the video list screen in the application, including the URL of the video that does not play.

Other

Q. Can't I get rid of ads through in-app purchases?
You can remove ads by becoming a supporter.
For more information, please tap the "Ad" button in the upper right corner of the video list screen.

Q. Do you accept Donate?
I accept from the tip link in the menu on the upper left.
